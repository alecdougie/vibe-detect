// How the Scoring Works
Websites are scored from 0 to 100
Higher score = more AI-generated signals detected
VERDICTS
0-19 — HUMAN CRAFTED
20-44 — AI ASSISTED
45-69 — MOSTLY VIBED
70-100 — PURE VIBES
CATEGORY CAPS
Each category has a max contribution to prevent any single area from dominating the score.

MODIFIERS
x1.15 Stack Multiplier
Applied when React + shadcn/Tailwind + Vercel are all detected together (the "holy trinity" of AI-generated sites)
Clean Signal Bonuses
Sites earn negative points for good practices: custom fonts, proper accessibility, semantic HTML, real content, and optimized performance
Final score = sum of capped categories × multiplier − clean bonuses, clamped to 0-100
